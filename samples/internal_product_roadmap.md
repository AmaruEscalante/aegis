## Executive Summary

Helix is targeting $42M ARR by end of FY2026 (up from $28M in FY2025). This roadmap outlines the three bets required to hit that number. We are prioritizing Enterprise expansion over SMB self-serve growth.

---

## Strategic Bets

### Bet 1: Enterprise Data Connectors (Q1–Q2 2026)
**Target ARR contribution: +$6M**

We will build native connectors for Salesforce, ServiceNow, and Microsoft Dynamics 365. Current enterprise deals are blocked 60% of the time by lack of CRM integration. This removes the primary sales blocker for the $50K+ ACV segment.

Investment: $1.8M (5 engineers, 2 quarters)
Risks: Salesforce API rate limits; ServiceNow certification process (avg. 4 months)
Owner: Priya Subramaniam, VP Engineering

### Bet 2: AI-Assisted Workflow Automation (Q2–Q3 2026)
**Target ARR contribution: +$4.5M (expansion revenue)**

Embed LLM-powered suggestions into the workflow builder. Targets 30% reduction in time-to-value for new customers and 15-point NPS improvement. Pricing: $15/seat/month add-on.

Investment: $2.2M (4 engineers + 1 ML researcher, 18 months)
Risks: LLM inference cost at scale; hallucination in production workflows
Owner: Marcus Chen, Head of AI Product

### Bet 3: Self-Serve Enterprise Trial (Q3 2026)
**Target ARR contribution: $3M (new logos)**

Remove sales-assisted trial requirement for companies under 200 seats. Current 45-day avg time-from-signup-to-paid must drop to under 18 days. PLG motion with automated upgrade triggers.

Investment: $900K (2 engineers, growth PM)
Owner: Sandra Vega, Head of Growth
