CONFIDENTIAL PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

Between Acme Corporation ("Company A") and TechNova Inc. ("Company B")
Effective Date: October 1, 2025

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

This agreement governs the joint development of "Project Titan" - a next-generation
autonomous drone delivery platform targeting the North American market. Project Titan
represents a $45 million investment over 18 months with an expected launch in Q2 2027.

2. REVENUE SHARING

Company A shall receive 60% of net revenues from Project Titan operations.
Company B shall receive 40% of net revenues from Project Titan operations.
Minimum guaranteed payment to Company B: $2.5 million per quarter post-launch.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All patents filed under Project Titan shall be jointly owned. The core flight
control algorithm ("Phoenix Engine v3") is proprietary to Company B and licensed
exclusively to this partnership for 5 years.

4. KEY PERSONNEL

Project Lead: Dr. Sarah Mitchell (Company A), employee ID: ACM-2847
Technical Director: James Park (Company B), employee ID: TN-0592
Board Liaison: Michael Torres, michael.torres@acmecorp.com, (415) 555-0199

5. NON-COMPETE

Neither party shall engage in autonomous delivery partnerships with Amazon,
Google Wing, or Zipline for the duration of this agreement plus 24 months.

6. TERMINATION CLAUSE

Early termination requires 90 days written notice and a penalty of $8 million
payable to the non-terminating party.
