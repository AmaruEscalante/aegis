SECTION 2: MANUFACTURING PROCESS NOTES (CONFIDENTIAL)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Step 1: Micronize Beta-Alanine to D50 < 12µm using Jet Mill #3 (NOT ball mill —
        causes 8% degradation observed in batch AFS-2023-Q3-004).

Step 2: Pre-blend all stimulant components (caffeine + theanine) in a 1:1 ratio
        with microcrystalline cellulose before combining with base blend. This
        controls hot spots that caused batch-to-batch variance in 2022.

Step 3: Final blend order: citrulline → betaine → BA → stimulant premix →
        absorption matrix → salt → flavor + color last.
        Deviation from order causes clumping (batch AFS-2024-Q1-002 was rejected).

Step 4: Final moisture target ≤ 0.8% (Karl Fischer). Reject above 1.1%.
