## Severance Offer

We are offering severance in exchange for a signed Separation Agreement
(attached). Acceptance is voluntary. The offered package:

- **Severance payment:** [N] weeks of base salary at your current rate,
  paid in a lump sum on the first regular pay date following your
  signing of the Separation Agreement and the expiration of the
  rescission period.
- **COBRA contribution:** Acme will pay your COBRA premium for [N]

## Your Continuing Obligations

You remain bound by your Confidentiality and Invention Assignment
Agreement, including obligations regarding confidential information,
non-solicitation of employees for 12 months following separation, and
assignment of any prior unassigned work product.

## Review Period and Rescission

You have **21 calendar days** to review the Separation Agreement before
signing. After signing, you have **seven (7) days** to revoke your
acceptance.

You are encouraged to consult with an attorney before signing.
