# 2025 Executive Compensation Committee Report
## Compensation Committee of the Board — Acme Corporation
### CONFIDENTIAL — BOARD COMPENSATION COMMITTEE EYES ONLY

**Date:** November 14, 2025
**Prepared by:** Priscilla Vega (Comp Committee Chair), with support
from Pearl Atkinson Advisors (independent compensation consultant)
**Distribution:** Compensation Committee members; CEO (sections 1-3
only)

---

## 1. Purpose

This report reviews 2025 executive compensation against our defined
peer group, recommends 2026 target compensation for each named
executive officer, and proposes equity grant sizing for the annual
refresh cycle.

## 2. Peer Group

Our peer group consists of 18 public and private SaaS companies with
ARR between $150M and $400M, headquartered in the United States. The
group is reviewed annually; this year we added two companies and
removed one. The full list is in Appendix A.

Benchmarks shown reflect the median and 65th percentile of the peer
group, weighted equally.

## 4. Benchmark Positioning

Against the 65th percentile of the peer group:

- D. Kowalski: total cash 96% of P65; equity 88% of P65 — slight
  market lag, justified by founder-status and existing equity holdings.
- M. Linehan: total cash 102%; equity 94%.
- T. Adisa: total cash 99%; equity 91%.
- O. Park: total cash 108%; equity 105% — currently above P65,
  reflecting the recruitment premium paid at hire.
- C. Westbrook: total cash 91%; equity 87% — meaningful gap;
  recommendation in Section 5.

## 5. Recommended 2026 Actions

### CEO (D. Kowalski)
- Base: $500,000 (5.3% increase)
- Target bonus: $500,000 (100%)
- Equity refresh: $5,000,000 grant value; 4-year vest, quarterly,
  no cliff (refresh standard)
- Performance equity overlay: $1,000,000 grant value, vesting on
  achievement of FY26 ARR > $300M

### CFO (M. Linehan)
- Base: $400,000 (3.9%)
- Target bonus: 60% ($240,000)
- Equity refresh: $1,800,000

### CTO (T. Adisa)
- Base: $415,000 (5.1%)
- Target bonus: 60% ($249,000)
- Equity refresh: $1,900,000

### General Counsel (O. Park)
- Base: $355,000 (2.9%)
- Target bonus: 50% ($177,500)
- Equity refresh: $900,000 (smaller; positioning correction)

### VP CS (C. Westbrook)
- Base: $315,000 (6.8%) — closing P65 gap
- Target bonus: 50% ($157,500)
- Equity refresh: $1,200,000 (above standard refresh; gap correction)
