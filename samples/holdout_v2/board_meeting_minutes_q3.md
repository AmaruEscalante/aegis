### 1. Call to Order

Helen Marston called the meeting to order at 9:04 AM. Quorum confirmed.
Minutes from the August 4 special meeting were approved unanimously.

### 2. CEO Report (D. Kowalski)

- Q3 ARR: $214.6M, ahead of plan ($210M). YoY growth: 38%.
- Net dollar retention: 119%, down 3 points QoQ — primarily due to two
  large logo expansions slipping into Q4.
- Headcount: 1,142 (vs. 1,098 at start of quarter). Engineering hiring
  bar tightened in late August; offer acceptance rate climbed to 71%.
- Customer Success org reorganization completed September 5. Early
  signals positive; full evaluation in Q4.

### 3. Financial Review (M. Linehan)

- GAAP gross margin: 76.4% (vs. 75.8% plan).
- Operating cash flow: $18.2M (vs. $14M plan); driven by improved
  collections.
- Cash position: $312M; runway > 36 months at current burn.
- FY25 guidance update reviewed; the board approved internal-only
  rolling 18-month forecast presented in the pre-read.

### 4. Audit Committee Report (P. Vega)

- Big-4 auditor selection process complete; recommendation to retain
  current auditor for FY26 approved.
- Materially weakness identified in Q2 (intercompany reconciliation)
  remediated per remediation plan; auditor signed off.

### 5. Strategic Discussion: Platform Bet

The board discussed the proposed platform investment outlined in the
August strategy memo. Key concerns surfaced:

- Halverson: opportunity cost vs. core ARR motion.
- Vega: gross margin trajectory if platform free tier is generous.
- Tanaka: hiring plan ramp realism.

Resolution: CEO to return at the November meeting with a phased
investment plan and explicit ARR-trade-off model.

### 6. Litigation Update (A. Devarajan, O. Park)

- Status update on the Pinemark Systems v. Acme matter. Discovery phase
  closes November 14. Trial date set for March 2026.
- Counsel's preliminary range of exposure unchanged from Q2.
- Discussion of settlement posture deferred to executive session.
