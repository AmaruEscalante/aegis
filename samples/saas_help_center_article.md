# How to Set Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**Last updated:** March 2024 | **Product:** Workspace Pro

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account. Even if someone knows your password, they won't be able to sign in without also having access to your authentication device.

---

## Before You Begin

You'll need:
- A smartphone with an authenticator app installed (Google Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password all work)
- Access to your account settings

---

## Enabling 2FA

1. Sign in to your account at **app.workspacepro.io**
2. Click your avatar in the top-right corner and select **Account Settings**
3. In the left sidebar, click **Security**
4. Under **Two-Factor Authentication**, click **Enable 2FA**
5. A QR code will appear on screen
6. Open your authenticator app and tap the **+** button (or "Add Account")
7. Scan the QR code with your phone's camera
8. Your authenticator app will display a 6-digit code — enter it in the **Verification Code** box on the website
9. Click **Confirm** to complete setup

Your account is now protected with 2FA.

---

## Logging In With 2FA Enabled

After entering your password, you'll be prompted for a verification code. Open your authenticator app, find the **Workspace Pro** entry, and enter the 6-digit code shown. Codes refresh every 30 seconds.

---

## Recovery Codes

During setup, you'll be shown 8 recovery codes. **Save these somewhere safe** (e.g., a password manager). Each code can be used once to sign in if you lose access to your authenticator app.

---

## Disabling 2FA

1. Go to **Account Settings → Security**
2. Under **Two-Factor Authentication**, click **Disable 2FA**
3. Enter your current password and a verification code to confirm

> **Note:** Disabling 2FA makes your account less secure. We recommend keeping it enabled at all times.

---

## Troubleshooting

**My code isn't working**
Authenticator apps rely on your phone's clock being accurate. Check that your phone's time is set to "automatic" or sync it manually.

**I lost my phone**
Use one of your recovery codes to sign in, then re-enroll with your new device.

**I never received recovery codes**
Contact support at help@workspacepro.io with your account email and we'll help you regain access.
