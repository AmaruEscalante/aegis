# [DRAFT] Org Change Announcement — Internal Only

**Status:** working draft, not for distribution
**Audience (when finalized):** all-staff email + Slack #announcements
**Author:** People Ops + CEO comms
**Target send time:** 2026-06-02, 09:00 PT

---

Hi team,

I'm writing to share a difficult decision the leadership team has made.

After the board meeting last Thursday and a hard look at our path to break-even, we are reducing the size of the company by approximately 14% — about 47 roles across Engineering, Marketing, and Customer Success. This decision is mine and the exec team's; it does not reflect the performance of any individual.

The affected teammates have been notified this morning and will receive an email from their manager with details on severance, equity acceleration, healthcare continuation, and immigration support. We'll be holding a closed all-hands at 11:00 PT today for those who remain.

For those who are leaving: we are deeply sorry. The decision was driven by our financial runway, not by your work. Severance is at minimum 12 weeks plus full health coverage through end of year. Anyone on a visa: our immigration partner has been retained at our expense for the duration of any transition.

For those who remain: there will be substantial reorganization. Engineering will consolidate from 5 teams to 3; Marketing will pause demand-gen and refocus on product marketing; CS will move to a tiered model. Updated org charts will land in your inboxes by end of day Wednesday.

I owe you transparency on the why. Our annualized burn was running at $4.1M/quarter against $9.7M of cash. We pursued the Series C aggressively for four months and the term sheets that came back required dilution and governance terms the board could not accept. Cutting now extends our runway by 14 months and gives us a credible path to profitability on existing customer growth.

I want to acknowledge what this asks of those who remain. We will be doing more with less for at least two quarters. I will not pretend otherwise. We will reset expectations on roadmap, on time off, and on hiring.

If you have questions, please reach out to your manager, to People Ops, or directly to me. I will be holding open Q&A every day this week.

— Anders
