# LETTER OF INTENT — ACQUISITION OF MERIDIAN ANALYTICS, INC.

**CONFIDENTIAL — NOT FOR DISTRIBUTION**
Date: April 14, 2026
Prepared by: Nexum Capital Partners LLC

---

## 1. Parties

**Buyer:** Nexum Capital Partners LLC ("Nexum" or "Buyer")
**Target:** Meridian Analytics, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Meridian" or "Target")
**Sellers:** Holders of 100% of issued and outstanding capital stock of Meridian ("Sellers")

---

## 2. Transaction Structure

Nexum proposes to acquire 100% of the outstanding equity of Meridian on the following basis:

- **Structure:** Stock acquisition (all-cash consideration)
- **Enterprise Value:** $47,500,000 (forty-seven million five hundred thousand dollars)
- **Adjustments:** Standard working capital peg of $2.8M; customary adjustments for cash, debt, and transaction expenses at close
- **Earnout:** Up to $5,000,000 contingent on ARR exceeding $12M in the 18 months post-close
- **Escrow:** 10% of purchase price held in escrow for 18 months for indemnification

---

## 3. Key Assumptions

This LOI is predicated on:
- Meridian's unaudited FY2025 revenue of $8.2M ARR and EBITDA of $1.1M being materially confirmed
- No undisclosed liabilities exceeding $250,000 in aggregate
- Retention of CEO Rafael Medina and CTO Yuki Tanaka for minimum 24 months post-close
