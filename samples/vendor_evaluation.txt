INTERNAL USE ONLY — Vendor Evaluation Report
Prepared by: Procurement Team | Date: February 2026

Subject: Cloud Infrastructure Provider Selection for Project Nightfall

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
We evaluated three vendors for our next-generation cloud migration (Project Nightfall).
This document contains proprietary pricing, internal benchmarks, and strategic positioning
that should not be shared externally.

VENDOR COMPARISON

1. AWS (Amazon Web Services)
   - Proposed pricing: $2.4M/year (18% discount from list)
   - Strengths: Broadest service catalog, strong ML/AI offerings
   - Weaknesses: Complex billing, vendor lock-in concerns
   - Internal benchmark: 847ms avg latency on our workload

2. Google Cloud Platform
   - Proposed pricing: $1.9M/year (25% discount, aggressive to win)
   - Strengths: Best-in-class data analytics, competitive pricing
   - Weaknesses: Smaller enterprise support team
   - Internal benchmark: 792ms avg latency on our workload

3. Azure
   - Proposed pricing: $2.1M/year (20% discount, bundled with E5 licenses)
   - Strengths: Best hybrid story, AD integration
   - Weaknesses: UI complexity, occasional reliability concerns
   - Internal benchmark: 823ms avg latency on our workload

RECOMMENDATION
GCP is recommended based on cost savings ($500K/year vs AWS) and superior performance
on our specific workload profile. However, this recommendation is contingent on
negotiating 24/7 premium support at no additional cost.
