# CRISIS COMMUNICATIONS PLAYBOOK
## Helix Platform Inc. — CONFIDENTIAL
### Version 3.1 | Updated: November 2025
### Distribution: Executive Team, Comms, Legal, IR Only

---

## Scenarios Covered

1. Major data breach / security incident
2. Service outage (>4 hours)
3. Executive misconduct allegation
4. Regulatory investigation or subpoena
5. Negative press story (material impact)

---

## SCENARIO 1: Data Breach / Security Incident

### Hour 0–1 (Immediate)
- **Incident Commander:** CISO Elena Vasquez (primary) / VP Eng (backup)
- Page on-call security team immediately
- Notify CEO Alexis Donahue and General Counsel Rachel O'Brien within 30 minutes
- DO NOT make any public statement until Legal has reviewed
- Preserve all system logs — issue litigation hold if breach appears intentional

### Hour 1–4 (Containment)
- Identify scope: affected systems, records, customers
- Activate external forensics firm (retained: Bishop Fox — contact: jreed@bishopfox.com, +1-602-XXX-XXXX)
- Determine breach notification trigger: >500 records = state notification laws apply
- Draft internal status page (NOT public-facing) for CS team

### Hour 4–24 (Assessment & Notification)
Notification thresholds:
- Board: Within 4 hours of confirmed breach
- Affected customers: As required by law (varies by state; CA = "expedient"; GDPR = 72 hours)
- Press: Proactive statement ONLY if breach is public or media have contacted us

**Approved spokespeople:** CEO, General Counsel. NO ONE ELSE speaks to media.

### Draft Statement Template (DO NOT RELEASE WITHOUT LEGAL APPROVAL)
> "Helix Platform takes the security of our customers' data seriously. We recently
> became aware of a security incident and immediately launched an investigation.
> We are working with external forensic experts and law enforcement. We will notify
> all affected customers and provide support. We apologize for any concern this causes."

---

## SCENARIO 2: Extended Service Outage (>4 hours)

- Post to status.helixplatform.com within 30 minutes of confirmed P1 (CEO/COO approval not required for status updates)
- Customer email if >6 hours: Pre-approved template in `comms/templates/outage-email.md`
- Media inquiry: "We are aware of an issue affecting some customers and our engineering team is working to resolve it. We will provide updates on our status page."
- Do not disclose root cause publicly until post-mortem is complete and Legal has reviewed
